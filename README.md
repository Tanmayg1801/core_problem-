## üëæ The Breach Entities

### *The Slime*

> "Low intelligence, high appetite."

* *Behavior:* Mindlessly rushes the player.
* *Tactics:* Easy to kill with a single sword swipe, but deadly if you get cornered by a swarm.

### *The Alien Soldier*

> "Elite vanguard of the crashing object."

* *Behavior:* Keeps its distance and fires high-velocity projectiles.
* *Tactics:* Has high health. Use your *Magic Spell* to chip away at them from afar or *Dash* in for a risky sword finisher.

---

## üõ†Ô∏è The Gear

* *The Sword:* High damage, close range. Your primary tool for clearing slimes.
* *The Spell:* Long-range projectile. Deals lower damage and has a *cooldown*, but essential for taking out Soldiers without getting hit.
* *The Dash:* A quick burst of speed. Use it to reposition or phase through incoming fire.
